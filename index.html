About

Impetus Student Society is a student body in the JSS Academy of Technical Education, Noida, which uniquely aims at launching a movement to turn good students into better professionals, so as to provide the corporate world with quality professionals and hence making a student’s induction into the industry much easier. Alike its motto is its aim: ‘bridging the gap’ between the avenues in the industry and an individual. Impetus lays initiative to introduce today’s academician to tomorrows executive.
